TURFAID

HOW TO PLAY

Hey!! please help our dear friend Todd 🐸 to reach his home. He can reach his destination only after crossing the road and the river. You can use ↑ up arrow , ↓ down arrow , ← left arrow and → right arrow on the keyboard to help him reach his destination. Make sure while crossing the road you never make him hit the cars 🚗and while crossing the river 🌊 you should help him not falling in to it by making him climb the logs and the tortoises 🐢 passing by. When you help Todd 🐸 reach his home you gain a score 💰.
